# Missing Product Images Report

Cross-reference of every SKU/MPN in `02 Current Catalog/updated_products.xlsx` against `image-mapping-table.csv` (04 Working Files) to identify which SKUs have no corresponding product image.

## Summary

- Total SKUs in the updated catalog: **1037**
- SKUs with at least one candidate image (exact, fuzzy, or ambiguous match): **121**
- SKUs with no image at all: **916**
- Total image files reviewed: 195 (173 matched to a SKU, 22 unmatched)

## Key finding

The image library in "03 Images" only covers liquid chemical products in a handful of container sizes (1 Gallon, 5 Gallon, 16 oz) — mostly RCP-branded concentrates/RTUs. It has essentially no coverage for equipment, pads, brushes, towels, dyes, packaging, or accessories, which make up the majority of the 1,037-SKU catalog. That is why 916 of 1,037 SKUs (88%) show up with no image.
