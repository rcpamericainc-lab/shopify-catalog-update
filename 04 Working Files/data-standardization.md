## Descriptions
The IK Multi Pro 12 is a professional-grade sprayer built for detailers and cleaning pros who demand performance, comfort, and safety across a wide range of jobs, from automotive detailing to industrial cleaning, disinfection services, and HVAC maintenance. Engineered to handle even the most aggressive chemicals, its heavy-duty construction and high resistance to acids make it a trusted choice for tough applications where standard sprayers fall short. Smooth pressurization and an ergonomic design keep it safe and easy to operate, even during extended use, while delivering consistent spray performance for reliable results every time. Whether you're applying wheel cleaners, degreasers, or disinfectants, the IK Multi PRO 12 gives you the durability and versatility to get the job done right. Paired with RCP's lineup of cleaners and detailing chemicals, this sprayer takes your professional workflow to the next level.
